FlickShare Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Welcome to FlickShare ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services to transfer files across platforms (Windows, Android, iOS, macOS).

1. No Personal Data Collection

FlickShare is designed as a direct, peer-to-peer (P2P) local file sharing tool. We do not collect, store, upload, or transmit any of your personal data or files to any external cloud servers. All files transferred using FlickShare remain entirely on your local devices.

2. How File Transfers Work

  • Local Wi-Fi Mode (P2P Socket): When you transfer files over local Wi-Fi, the connection is established directly between your devices on the same local network. The data never leaves your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Remote P2P Mode (WebRTC): When you use Remote P2P, we use a temporary signaling server strictly to connect the two devices. Once the connection is established, all files are sent directly between the devices using WebRTC peer-to-peer data channels. No files or contents are ever stored on our signaling servers.
  • Web Bridge Mode: When you host a Web Bridge (e.g. for sharing with macOS/iOS browsers), a temporary HTTP server runs locally on your device. Other devices on the same Wi-Fi connect directly to this local address. No external servers are involved.

3. Permissions Required & How They Are Used

To provide file transfer capabilities, FlickShare may ask for the following permissions on your device:

  • Network & Wi-Fi Access: Required to discover and connect to other devices on the same local network.
  • Storage (Read/Write): Required to select files to send and to save received files into your "Downloads" directory.
  • Camera Access (Optional): Required solely to scan QR codes to instantly establish local or remote P2P connections. We do not capture or store any photos or videos.

4. Data Security

Since your files are sent directly from device to device without passing through intermediary servers, your transfers are exceptionally secure and private from outside interception.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, our application is safe for all users, including children, and fully complies with child safety regulations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us
